# Privacy Policy for Yumli

**Last Updated:** May 2, 2026

Yumli ("the App") is developed by Viktor Ilin, an independent developer based in Chernivtsi, Ukraine ("I", "me", "my"). This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. What Data Is Collected

**Information you provide:**
- Dietary preferences (diet type, allergies, food dislikes)
- Meal plan settings (number of meals per day, plan duration, optional calorie target)
- Feedback you send through the App

**Information collected automatically:**
- Device type, operating system version, language
- Anonymous usage data (features used, crashes, performance)
- Advertising ID (only if you allow tracking)

I do **not** collect your name, email (unless you contact me), location, photos, contacts, or health data.

---

## 2. How Your Data Is Used

- To generate personalized meal plans based on your preferences
- To process Yumli Pro subscriptions
- To show ads to free tier users
- To fix bugs and improve the App

I do **not** sell your data.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses these services. Each has its own privacy policy:

- **Firebase (Google)** — stores recipes and meal plans. [Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **xAI / Grok** — receives your dietary preferences to select meals. No identifying info is sent. [Policy](https://x.ai/legal/privacy-policy)
- **RevenueCat** — manages Pro subscriptions. [Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- **Google AdMob** — shows ads to free users. [Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)
- **Apple** — handles in-app purchases. [Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

Your data may be processed in countries outside Ukraine, including the EU and the United States, where these services operate.

---

## 4. Your Data, Your Choices

Your meal plans are stored on your device and can be deleted anytime via "Delete Menus" in the App.

You have the right to:
- Access the data I hold about you
- Correct or delete it
- Opt out of ad tracking (iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking)
- Subscribe to Pro to remove ads entirely

**EU/EEA users:** Under GDPR, you also have the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

**Ukrainian users:** Under Ukrainian law (ЗУ "Про захист персональних даних"), you may contact the Ukrainian Parliament Commissioner for Human Rights (ombudsman.gov.ua) regarding data protection concerns.

To exercise any of these rights, email me at **yumli.support@gmail.com**.

---

## 5. Children

Yumli is not intended for users under 13. If you believe a child has used the App, contact me and I will delete their data.

If I update this policy, I will change the "Last Updated" date at the top. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated version.
